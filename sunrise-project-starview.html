Starview

A contemporary high-desert home at Brasada Ranch, open to the light, the lawn, and the wide Cascade horizon.

Clean lines, warm materials, and glass that pulls the high desert inside.

Within the gates of Brasada Ranch in Powell Butte, Starview is a contemporary custom home built for light and view. Vaulted ceilings, exposed beams, and expansive glazing open the great room and kitchen to the covered patio and the Cascade horizon, while a warm, natural palette keeps everything grounded and calm.

It's a home for the way this rare place is lived — resort amenities at the doorstep, the quiet of the high desert all around, and indoor-outdoor living made for long Central Oregon evenings.
