What it takes

Every community has its own rules. We know them.

The finest sites here sit inside communities that hold their own standards — and a home that ignores them stalls in review or sits awkwardly in its setting. We've been through these processes, and we know the people who administer them, so your home moves smoothly from approval to finished.

See the process
  • Design review & guidelines
    Height, massing, materials, and color palettes that each community expects.
  • Architectural review committees
    Submittals and approvals handled cleanly, with relationships that help.
  • Setbacks, lots & siting
    Placing the home to protect views, light, and privacy within the rules.
  • HOA & build logistics
    Construction windows, access, and neighbor courtesies, managed for you.
