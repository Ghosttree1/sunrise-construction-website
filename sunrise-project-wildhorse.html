Wildhorse

A clean-lined contemporary home set into the high desert at Brasada Ranch — open, light, and built for the view.

Location
Brasada Ranch, Powell Butte
Style
Contemporary High-Desert Home
Community
Brasada Ranch
Setting
High desert · Cascade views

Crisp lines, warm finishes, and wide glass give this Brasada Ranch home a clean contemporary edge without losing the warmth of the high desert.

Set within Brasada Ranch, this contemporary home was designed to live with its surroundings — open living spaces that flow to covered outdoor rooms, a fireplace anchoring the great room, and expansive glazing that frames the Cascades and the wide Central Oregon sky.

It's a study in restraint done warmly: a modern home that still feels at home on the ranch, with the finish and detail that set a custom Sunrise build apart.
