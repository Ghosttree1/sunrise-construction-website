Our Legacy

It started on a ranch in Tumalo.

Twenty-six years later, the same pair of hands is still on every job site — and still building like it matters.

Some builders learn the trade. Chris Christianson grew up in it.

On his family's ranch outside Tumalo, a young Chris spent his days turning timber into tree forts — and, as he grew, into barns. The dreamer's hands became a craftsman's. At twenty, he turned that instinct into a livelihood, founding Sunrise in 2000 with a framing crew and a simple belief: that how a thing is built matters as much as how it looks when it's done.

What began with framing grew into light custom homes, and in time into the bespoke residences Sunrise is known for today — homes that have earned a place among Central Oregon's finest. Through every step, one thing never changed: Chris on site, hands on, answerable for the work.

Twenty-six years on, Sunrise is a premier Central Oregon builder. But ask Chris and he'll tell you the company still carries the soul of the boy on the ranch — the one who understood that building was never just about wood and nails, but about creating something beautiful and lasting.

"Make sure you're doing it right when no one is looking."

Chris Christianson · Founder & Builder
Limited by design

We take on fewer homes so each one gets more of us.

Sunrise is intentionally selective about the number of homes we build each year. That allows Chris to stay closely involved with each client and each project — without leaving the work dependent on one person.

Behind every Sunrise home is a coordinated team of project managers, superintendents, bookkeeping support, interior designers, lighting design, site protection, cleanup crews, trusted trades, and long-standing vendor relationships. The client relationship still runs directly through Chris, but the day-to-day execution is supported by the systems, staff, and buying power of an established builder.

"I stay personally involved because that's where the trust is built. But the reason our projects run well is the team behind them — the people, systems, trades, and details working together every day."
