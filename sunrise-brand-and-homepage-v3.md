# Sunrise Construction of Oregon — Brand & Homepage Direction (v3, corrected)

Brand center: **the client, the custom home, the experience, and the hands-on builder relationship.** Sunrise is a distinctive Central Oregon custom home builder — not an architecture studio, not land-only, not "by introduction only." Elevated and polished, but welcoming. Capable of many styles. Built around the client's vision.

---

## 1. Corrected brand positioning

**Positioning statement (primary):**
Distinctive custom homes in Central Oregon — built around your vision and guided by a better building experience.

**Stronger variations to choose from:**
- One of Central Oregon's top custom home builders — distinctive homes, built around your vision and a better experience from first idea to final detail.
- Distinctive custom homes in Central Oregon, built around you: your vision, your style, your budget, your life — guided start to finish by a hands-on builder.
- We build distinctive custom homes in Central Oregon the way they should be built — around your vision, in your style, with a process that's actually a pleasure.

**Positioning pillars (what every page should reinforce):**
1. **Built around the client, not a house style.** We execute bold, modern, traditional, mountain, ranch, lodge, transitional, or show-home — your vision leads, our experience guides.
2. **A better building experience.** Hands-on, communicative, organized. The process is as distinctive as the home.
3. **Real construction experience.** We guide without taking over. We've solved the problems before.
4. **Detail and craftsmanship.** The difference shows up in the finish, the selections, and the way it all comes together.
5. **Central Oregon rooted.** Communities, golf and resort properties, ranch and estate land, and one-of-a-kind lots — we know how to build here.
6. **A resource, not just a contractor.** For homeowners, out-of-area buyers, realtors, architects, and designers.
7. **High-end, but approachable.** You don't have to be building a forever estate to build with Sunrise.

**Elevator version:** Sunrise builds distinctive custom homes across Central Oregon, designed around how you actually want to live — and backed by a building experience that's organized, hands-on, and genuinely enjoyable, from first idea to long after move-in.

## 2. Corrected brand voice

**Personality:** confident, warm, and grounded. The expert who makes you feel capable, not small. Premium without being precious. Plainspoken without being plain.

**We sound:** client-first, encouraging, specific, proud of the work, calm under pressure.
**We do not sound:** exclusive, aloof, moody, architect-academic, salesy, or like every other contractor.

**Do:**
- Lead with "you" and "your." The home is the client's; we're the guide.
- Be concrete: selections, finishes, framing, the walk-through, the final detail.
- Show range: many styles, many communities, many kinds of clients.
- Make luxury feel reachable — confident invitation, not a velvet rope.
- Let real work and real people carry the prestige.

**Don't:**
- Don't lead with land, legacy, restraint, exclusivity, or "the drawing."
- Don't force one aesthetic or imply we only build moody/minimal homes.
- Don't over-claim or hard-sell. Earn it with specifics.
- Avoid tired contractor filler: *quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, dream home, turnkey, second to none* — unless genuinely re-earned and made specific.

**Words that fit:** distinctive, custom, vision, guided, hands-on, experience, crafted, considered, Central Oregon, your style, brought together, from concept to completion.
**Words to retire:** dream home, world-class, unparalleled, luxury (as filler), turnkey, one-stop, full-service.

**Tagline options:**
- Specializing in your vision.
- Built around your vision.
- Distinctive custom homes, built around you.
- Your vision. Brought together.

**Lead with Concept 1 — "Built Around You,"** with the **subheadline and proof-band from Concept 6 ("A better way to build")** layered in below the fold.

Why: it's the clearest, warmest expression of the corrected brand center (client first), it's instantly understandable, it carries "distinctive" and "guided" without any exclusivity, and it leaves room for every supporting idea — style range (4), Central Oregon (5), process/experience (3, 6), and craftsmanship (10) — to live as sections beneath it.

**Recommended hero:**

- Primary CTA: **Start your home**

Keep Concepts 4 ("however you picture it") and 8 ("personally built") as strong A/B test challengers — 4 if analytics show style-range is the top client question, 8 if the personal/hands-on relationship is the strongest sales driver.

## 7. Visual direction (real Sunrise work)

The brand experience should be carried by **real Sunrise homes, details, people, and process — not abstract graphics.** Photography is the design.

**Imagery hierarchy:**
- **Finished spaces** — warm, lived-in, light-filled great rooms, kitchens, primary suites, and outdoor living, across multiple styles.
- **Craftsmanship details** — joinery, cabinetry, stone, tile, steel-and-timber, hardware, transitions; proof of the finish.
- **Selections** — material palettes, samples, the design studio moment; makes the experience tangible and aspirational.
- **Process & people** — candid (but polished) shots of the Sunrise team with clients, on-site reviews, framing and construction moments. Humanizes the build.
- **Central Oregon lifestyle & setting** — homes in their communities and landscapes (golf, resort, ranch, view lots) shown as backdrop to living, never as the lead subject.

**Art direction:**
- Warm, natural light; true-to-life color; avoid cold, over-processed, or moody-dark grading.
- Show range deliberately — curate so different styles read as versatility, not inconsistency.
- Include people and life where possible; this is approachable luxury, not an empty showroom.
- Generous whitespace, refined serif/sans pairing, restrained motion. Premium polish, not flashy effects.
- Real over stock, always. Where galleries aren't ready, use the best available Sunrise project photography and plan a proper shoot (finished homes + a process/lifestyle day).

**Shot list to commission if gaps exist:** 3–5 finished homes across distinct styles; a craftsmanship detail set; a design/selections session; a client + builder on-site moment; one signature dusk "lights on" exterior per featured home.

## 8. SEO direction

Target the searches qualified Central Oregon clients actually use, centered on **custom home builder + client experience + place + communities** — without keyword stuffing. Write for people first; let the terms fall naturally into real sentences.

**Primary terms:** custom home builder Central Oregon · Central Oregon custom homes · luxury custom home builder Bend / Central Oregon · custom home builder Bend Oregon.

**Secondary / supporting terms:** custom home builder Redmond / Sisters / Sunriver / Tetherow / Pronghorn / Brasada Ranch · luxury home communities Central Oregon · ranch and estate home builder Central Oregon · golf community custom homes · resort community home builder · home building process Central Oregon · custom home design and selections.

**Page-level mapping:**
- **Home** — "Distinctive custom homes in Central Oregon" + custom home builder + client experience.
- **Build in Central Oregon** — area/place terms (Bend, Redmond, Sisters, Sunriver).
- **Luxury Communities** — named-community terms (one section or sub-page per major community).
- **Ranch & Estate Properties** — ranch/estate/acreage home builder terms.
- **The Process / Design + Selections** — "custom home building process," "home design and selections."
- **Selected Work** — style + place terms in real captions (e.g., "modern mountain custom home in Tetherow").
- **For Realtors / Architects & Designers** — referral and partner intent terms.

**On-page mechanics:**
- One clear H1 per page using the natural target phrase; descriptive H2s.
- Title tags ~55–60 chars, meta descriptions ~150 chars, both human and specific (e.g., *Custom Home Builder in Central Oregon | Sunrise Construction*).
- Real, descriptive alt text on every project image (style + place).
- Local SEO: complete Google Business Profile, consistent NAP, service-area pages for Bend/Redmond/Sisters/Sunriver, LocalBusiness/HomeBuilder schema.
- Each luxury community as its own indexed page captures high-intent named searches.
- Genuine, helpful long-form on Building Resources (cost, timeline, choosing a builder, building in a community) earns informational traffic and trust.

**Avoid:** repeating "custom home builder Central Oregon" unnaturally, thin doorway pages per town, and hidden keyword blocks. Depth and specificity rank; stuffing doesn't.
