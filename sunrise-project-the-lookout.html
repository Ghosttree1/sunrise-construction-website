The Lookout

A butte-top home in Powell Butte with a 270-degree sweep of mountains — from Smith Rock to Paulina Peak.

Setting

View

Style

Built for the view — 270 degrees of Central Oregon skyline.

The Lookout sits high on a butte in Powell Butte, where the land falls away on every side and the horizon opens to a 270-degree sweep of mountains — from the spires of Smith Rock across the Cascades to Paulina Peak. The home is planned around that panorama, with living spaces and outdoor rooms turned to the light and the long views.

Inside, the palette stays quiet and warm so nothing competes with the setting. It's a home that earns its name — a place built to take in the whole of Central Oregon at once.
