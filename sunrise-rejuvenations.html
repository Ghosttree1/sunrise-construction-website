Rejuvenations

Some homes deserve a second life.

New custom homes are our heart — but each year we take on a select few whole-home rejuvenations, reimagining an existing house into something extraordinary.

Reimagining, not remodeling

A rejuvenation is a custom home, drawn out of the one you already have.

This isn't a quick remodel or a bolt-on addition. A Sunrise rejuvenation is a top-to-bottom reimagining — rethinking how a home lives, opening it to light and view, renewing its structure and systems, and rebuilding it with the same craft, detail, and finish as a ground-up custom home. We take on only a few each year, and we pour everything into them.

What a rejuvenation includes
▢

Whole-home reimagination

Floor plan, flow, light, and proportion — rethought from the inside out, not just refreshed.

⚒

Structure & systems renewed

Framing, mechanicals, windows, and envelope brought fully up to a modern custom standard.

+

Additions that belong

New space woven in so seamlessly you'd never know where the original home ends.

✻

The full design experience

The same Sunrise Interiors finishes, selections, and detail that go into our new homes.

☉

Built for the setting

We open the home to the views and the Central Oregon light its lot was made for.

⚷

The same care after

Warranty, care, and The Key — your rejuvenated home looked after for years to come.
