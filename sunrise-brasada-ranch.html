Few places in Central Oregon live like Brasada Ranch — and few builders know how to build here the way we do.

Sunrise Construction is a custom home builder at Brasada Ranch, the gated high-desert resort community spread across Powell Butte, just east of Bend. We build genuinely bespoke homes here: your ideas brought to life, not forced into a stock plan. Every project is a close collaboration between architect, designer, owner, and builder — and our own owner, Chris, is personally involved from the first idea to the final detail.

That way of working matters more at Brasada than most places. Bringing us in early, alongside your architect and designer, keeps the whole process considered from the very first conversation.

Why here

Why Build in Brasada Ranch?

Brasada Ranch offers something increasingly rare in Central Oregon: the feeling of being removed from everything while remaining connected to it. Perched above the high desert with sweeping Cascade views, the community blends open space, luxury amenities, and thoughtfully designed neighborhoods into a place that feels more like a destination than a subdivision.

For many of our clients, the decision to build here isn't driven by golf or resort amenities alone. It's about waking to uninterrupted views, enjoying quiet evenings under dark skies, and creating a home that becomes part of the landscape rather than simply sitting on it. Brasada encourages architecture that respects the land, rewards thoughtful design, and embraces the indoor-outdoor lifestyle that makes Central Oregon so special.

Every homesite presents different opportunities. Some capture dramatic mountain panoramas, while others offer privacy among the junipers or overlook the rolling high desert terrain. Orienting the home correctly, preserving the best sightlines, and designing for Central Oregon's sun, wind, snow, and seasonal climate all influence how the home will feel for decades to come.

Just as important is understanding the community's architectural review process before design begins. Bringing the builder into the conversation early allows the architect, designer, and homeowner to work together from the start, creating a home that fits both your vision and the character of Brasada Ranch while helping avoid unnecessary revisions later in the process.

After building more than a dozen custom homes within Brasada Ranch, we've learned that the best homes here are never defined by square footage or finishes alone. They're defined by how naturally they belong to the land, how comfortably they support everyday living, and how timeless they remain long after construction is complete.

Good questions

Frequently asked questions.

Can anyone build a custom home in Brasada Ranch?

Yes. Property owners may choose the builder that best fits their project, provided the home complies with the community's architectural review requirements. Selecting a builder with experience in Brasada Ranch can make the planning and approval process significantly smoother.

Are you an approved builder in Brasada Ranch?

Absolutely. Sunrise Construction is an approved builder within Brasada Ranch and has completed custom homes throughout the community, including work performed directly for Brasada Ranch itself. We understand the architectural review process, community standards, and the level of planning required to move projects smoothly from concept through construction.

Our clients choose us because of our experience, craftsmanship, and collaborative approach—not because of a referral list. We believe homeowners should select the builder who is the right fit for their vision, and we're honored that many Brasada homeowners find us through past clients, architects, designers, and our completed work within the community.

When should I involve Sunrise Construction?

Earlier than most people think. Bringing your builder into the conversation before architectural plans are finalized allows us to help evaluate the homesite, coordinate with your architect and designer, anticipate construction costs, and identify opportunities that may improve the design while avoiding costly revisions later.

Do I have to use your architect or interior designer?

No. Some clients already have an architect, interior designer, or completed plans, while others are starting from the very beginning. Sunrise is flexible enough to meet you wherever you are in the process.

If you're assembling your team, we'll recommend architects whose design philosophy, communication style, and personality are the right fit for your project—not simply the same architect for every home. We believe the relationship between homeowner and architect is just as important as the plans themselves.

One of Sunrise Construction's unique advantages is our in-house interior design team through Sunrise Interiors. Because interior design and construction work together from the beginning, selections, lighting, cabinetry, finishes, budgets, and construction details are coordinated early, creating a smoother building experience and a more cohesive home.

What if I already have plans or an architect?

That's perfectly fine. We regularly build homes designed by architects selected by our clients and are equally comfortable joining projects that already have completed construction documents.

Our role is to become a collaborative member of your team—working closely with your architect, interior designer, engineers, consultants, and trades to help move the project from drawings to a beautifully built home.

Whether your plans are just beginning or already permit-ready, Sunrise adapts to your process rather than asking you to adapt to ours.

Can Sunrise renovate an existing Brasada Ranch home?

Absolutely. In addition to building new custom homes, we've completed numerous whole-home rejuvenations within Brasada Ranch. Whether updating finishes, reconfiguring living spaces, modernizing kitchens, or transforming an older home into something that better fits your lifestyle, we approach renovation with the same level of craftsmanship as new construction.

How long does it take to build a custom home in Brasada Ranch?

Every project is unique, but most luxury custom homes require thoughtful planning well before construction begins. Site evaluation, design, engineering, architectural review, permitting, and selections all influence the overall schedule. We develop realistic timelines early in the process so expectations remain clear from the beginning.

What architectural styles work best in Brasada Ranch?

Brasada Ranch accommodates a wide range of architectural styles, from mountain contemporary and modern ranch to timber-and-stone estates and refined Northwest modern homes. The most successful homes are those that respond naturally to their surroundings, maximize views, and feel like they belong to the landscape.

What makes building in Brasada Ranch different from other Central Oregon communities?

The combination of dramatic topography, expansive views, architectural review standards, and resort lifestyle creates opportunities—and responsibilities—that don't exist on many homesites. Designing specifically for the lot, the climate, and the way you'll live in the home is what separates an exceptional Brasada Ranch home from one that could have been built anywhere.

Do you only build in Brasada Ranch?

No. Sunrise Construction builds custom homes throughout Central Oregon, including Bend, Juniper Preserve, Caldera Springs, and other premier communities. Brasada Ranch is simply one of the places where we've had the privilege of building for many years.
