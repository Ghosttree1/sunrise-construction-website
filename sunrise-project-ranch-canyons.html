Cliff Side Ranch Estate

A Tuscan-inspired estate above the Crooked River canyon, in one of Central Oregon's most distinctive gated communities.

Old-world warmth on the rim of the high desert — an estate that belongs to its dramatic setting.

Set within Ranch at the Canyons, the gated estate community perched above the Crooked River canyon near Smith Rock, this home draws on Tuscan and Mediterranean tradition — warm stone and timber, arched forms, and a courtyard sensibility — while opening fully to the wide high-desert views the site is known for.

It's a home built for the way this rare place is lived: vineyards and canyon trails outside, and refined, hand-detailed living within.

Featured in BUILD Magazine and Bend Magazine.
