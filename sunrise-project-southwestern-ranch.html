Warm adobe tones, deep shade, and the red rock of Smith Rock right on the horizon.

Set on the high desert near Smith Rock in Terrebonne, this Southwestern-style estate trades mountain-modern for something warmer and older — territorial forms, earthen tones, timber and stone, and shaded outdoor rooms made for the desert light. It sits into the landscape as though it has always been there.

It's a true equestrian property, and Sunrise built the whole place — the residence and the barn — with the same craft and detail across both. The result is a working ranch that lives like a retreat, with one of Central Oregon's most iconic views as its backdrop.
