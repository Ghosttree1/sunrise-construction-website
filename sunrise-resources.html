A resource, not just a builder.

For homeowners, realtors, architects, designers, and buyers from out of the area — Sunrise is a resource you can lean on before, during, and long after the build.

How we help

Good building takes a good team around it.

Over 26 years we've become a trusted partner to the people who guide our clients — and a steady resource for the clients themselves. However you come to us, here's how we make the work easier.

For realtors

A builder partner who protects your relationship.

Send us the client weighing raw land against a build, or one who needs a straight read on what a lot can become and what it will truly cost. We make you look good and keep the relationship yours.

  • Honest lot and feasibility reads for your buyers
  • Line-item budgets that set realistic expectations early
  • A smooth, communicative build your clients will thank you for

For architects & designers

An execution partner who protects the drawing.

When a design can't afford to lose anything in the field, we're the builder who keeps it intact — collaborating cleanly with your team and getting subcontractors to "yes," detail by detail.

  • Faithful execution from rendering to finished room
  • Seamless collaboration with your design and selections
  • Problem-solvers who protect intent under real-world constraints

For out-of-area buyers

Build in Central Oregon from anywhere.

Many of our clients build from out of town. Live job-site cameras and our project management app keep you connected to every update, decision, and milestone — so distance never means being out of the loop.

  • Live job-site cameras and real-time progress
  • Our complete project management app for selections, budget, and approvals
  • Help finding the right lot and community before you arrive
