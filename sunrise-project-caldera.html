Resort ease, in the trees — a home made for gathering and for getting away.

Set within Caldera Springs, the forested resort community south of Bend near Sunriver, this home was built for the relaxed rhythm of resort life: open, light-filled living, seamless indoor-outdoor flow, and warm Northwest materials that settle into the pines and lava-rock surroundings.

Lakes, trails, and the full amenity set of the Sunriver area are just outside — lock-and-leave living without giving up a single custom detail.
